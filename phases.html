SDLC

Software development life cycle

Planning and Requirement Analysis

This stage is performed by the senior members and with input from the customer, which is then used to plan the basic project approach

Defining Requirements

At this stage the products requirements are defined and approved by the customer.

Designing the Product Architecture

At this stage the products architecture is defined.

Building or Developing the Product

At this stage the product is developed by the developers, whom must follow the coding guidelines defined by their organization and programming tools.

Testing the Product

At this stage the product is tested and fixed to be ready for the deployment.

Deployment in the Market and Maintenance

At this stage the product is deployed and maintained according to the feedback that the product is getting.
